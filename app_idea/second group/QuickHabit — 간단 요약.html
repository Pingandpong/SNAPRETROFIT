기술 스택

  • RN/Expo: expo-notifications, expo-sharing, expo-localization, @react-native-async-storage/async-storage, react-native-svg
  • Firebase: Auth(익명), Firestore(기록), Analytics, Remote Config(A/B)
  • UI: Gluestack UI(Card/Checkbox/Button/Badge)
  • MCP: KR→EN 번역, 짧은 동기 문구, ASO 카피 생성
